- ## Coaching Thresholds

  - ### Overcommitting Signals (Pull Back)
    - Daily scheduled hours > 6
    - Working past 9pm > 2 days/week
    - Exercise sessions < target
    - No family time events
    - No buffer time between blocks
    - Weekend work becoming regular

  - ### Slacking Signals (Push)
    - ONE Thing not done 3+ days
    - Same task carried over 3+ times
    - Inbox > 10 items
    - Deep work < 3 hours/day average
    - No goal/milestone progress
    - Morning hours wasted
