- ## The 3 Rules
  - **Rule 1**: Capture fast, organize later
  - **Rule 2**: One place for thoughts (Inbox), one place for today (Journal)
  - **Rule 3**: If it takes more than 5 seconds, you're overcomplicating it

- ## Daily Workflow (5 minutes total)

- ### Morning (2 min)
  - Open today's journal
  - Type `/template` ‚Üí select `daily`
  - Replace "Task 1" with your ONE most important thing
  - Add 2-3 more tasks (or skip if your day is reactive)
  - Done. Start working.

- ### During the Day (10 seconds each)
  - **Thought pops up?** ‚Üí Open [[Inbox]], type it, close
  - **Task done?** ‚Üí Tap the checkbox (TODO ‚Üí DONE)
  - **New task?** ‚Üí Add to today's journal: `- TODO thing [[Project]]`
  - **Big idea?** ‚Üí Dump in [[Inbox]], process tonight

- ### Evening (2 min, optional)
  - Glance at journal ‚Üí note what got done
  - Check [[Inbox]] ‚Üí move items to proper pages or tomorrow
  - That's it. Close the app.

- ## Real World Scenarios

- ### Scenario 1: Random thought while commuting
  **Situation**: You're on the train and remember you need to renew car insurance.

  **Do this**:
  - Open Logseq ‚Üí [[Inbox]]
  - Type: `renew car insurance`
  - Close app

  **Time**: 5 seconds

  **Later** (evening): Move to [[Finances]] or tomorrow's journal with `- TODO Renew car insurance [[Finances]]`

- ### Scenario 4: Overwhelmed with too many things
  **Situation**: Your brain is full, you feel anxious, too many tabs open in your head.

  **Do this**:
  - Open [[Inbox]]
  - Brain dump EVERYTHING (no formatting, no thinking, just type)
  - One item per line

  **Later** (when calm): Process inbox, move items to projects or delete

  **Situation**: Want to set up the week ahead.

  - Check [[Goals]] and [[Projects]]
  - Identify 3-5 key outcomes for the week
  - Done

  **Time**: 10 minutes

- ## Power User Tips

- ### Quick Capture Shortcuts
  - `- TODO ` starts a task
  - `[[` opens page linking (autocomplete shows existing pages)
  - `/template` inserts templates
  - `DONE` or checkbox marks complete

- ### Link Everything to Projects
  - Bad: `- TODO Fix the bug`
  - Good: `- TODO Fix the bug [[MVP Launch]]`
  - Why: Claude can find all tasks for a project, you can see related work

- ### Use Inbox as RAM, Journal as Today
  - **Inbox** = temporary holding, brain dump, random thoughts
  - **Journal** = today's commitments, what you're actually doing
  - Never let Inbox grow beyond 10 items (process daily)

- ### The 2-Minute Rule
  - If a task takes less than 2 minutes, do it now
  - Don't write it down, just do it
  - Only capture things that need tracking

- ### Don't Over-Organize
  - You don't need folders
  - You don't need tags (project links are enough)
  - You don't need priorities (your ONE thing is the priority)
  - Keep it flat, keep it simple

- ## What NOT to Do

- ### Don't
  - Spend 20 minutes "setting up" your day
  - Create complex hierarchies and nested projects
  - Feel guilty about an empty journal
  - Track everything (Streaks handles habits)
  - Use this for reference material (use Google Drive/Notion for that)

- ### Do
  - Capture fast, think later
  - Trust the system (it's searchable)
  - Keep Inbox clean
  - Focus on ONE thing per day
  - Let Claude do the heavy lifting (morning briefing)
