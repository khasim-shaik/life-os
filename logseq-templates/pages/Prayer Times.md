- ## Calendar Blocking Rules
  - Each prayer = 15-minute block (minimum)
  - Fajr: Non-negotiable wake time
  - Dhuhr/Asr: Schedule meetings around these
  - Maghrib: Short window - be available
  - Isha: Can be flexible but don't delay too late

- ## Ramadan Adjustments
  - Fajr becomes earlier (before Suhoor ends)
  - All times shift with longer fasting hours
  - Take entire month off from heavy work
  - Focus on worship, family, reflection
